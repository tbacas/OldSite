Work

Predicting Bond Prices with Machine Learning

Neural Networks and Predictive Modeling

Unlike the stock market, bonds have uncharacteristically less information available for traders. Equity traders have current and historic trade data available quickly and cost effectively while Bond traders suffer from information drought. This leads to bond prices being significantly less effeciant than those of equity market. With this in mind this project sought to develop a model to produce more accurate pricing predictions for bonds. Utilizing data from Benchmark Solutions and Neural Networks/Ensemble models this product can predict accurate and computationally effecient bond prices.

Zillow Housing Valuation for Damage Estimates

GeoPandas and API Utilization

New Light Technologies, a contractor for FEMA requested dynamic code for producing housing estimates for a given zipcode. During a disaster, it is important to model and estimate the potential or forecasted effect of the event, including the projected/forecasted damage. FEMA has already developed estimates for other factors, this project seeks to add an additional indicator, up-to-date estimates for property value in the area of effect. Utilizing Zillow API this product allows for quick return on property statistics of an affected area or zip-code.

Webscraping Financial Statements

Webscraping and Pandas

Working at a mutual fund I found a major bandwith issue in finding finacial statements for companies the fund was researching. Using Pandas Dataframes and MorningStar API this project sought to download several thousand financial statements from hundreads of different companies quickly and effectively. Running 5 years of financial statements for the entire SP 500 takes no more than 5 minutes. The current product includes CSV conversion for individuals intrestested in doing their analysis in Excel.

Determining Reddit Subreddit by Posts

Natural Language Processing and Category Models

Using Natural Language Processing and the content and comments of posts on reddit this project could determine which subreddit a post originated. Further implimentation could determine which posts gain increased karma based on their content and title. The examples used in this project were /r Changemyview and /r Finance. The model was able to locate the most important terms for each subreddit (in the case of /r Finance "Risk").
